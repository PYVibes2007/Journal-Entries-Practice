Assets

DEBIT Increases: Cash received, equipment purchased, etc.
CREDIT Decreases: Cash paid, supplies used, etc.

Examples: Cash, Accounts Receivable, Equipment, Supplies

Liabilities

DEBIT Decreases: Paying off debts
CREDIT Increases: Borrowing money, buying on account

Examples: Accounts Payable, Notes Payable, Unearned Revenue

Equity

DEBIT Decreases: Owner withdrawals
CREDIT Increases: Owner investments

Examples: Izzy Summerbell, Capital (credit); Izzy Summerbell, Withdrawal (debit)

Revenue

DEBIT Decreases: Refunds or corrections
CREDIT Increases: Earning revenue, receiving payments

Examples: Fees Earned, Rent Revenue, Service Revenue

Expenses

DEBIT Increases: Recording expenses incurred
CREDIT Decreases: Correcting overstated expenses

Examples: Rent Expense, Salaries Expense, Utilities Expense
